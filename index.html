About Me

From the Lab
to the App

I'm a pharmacy graduate from ISTN Jakarta, passionate about bridging the gap between pharmaceutical science and digital health. My journey began in compounding labs and community pharmacies — places where I saw first-hand how medication errors, missed doses, and poor health literacy affect patients every day.

That experience planted a seed: what if technology could extend the pharmacist's reach beyond the counter? I started exploring iOS development fundamentals and realized that my clinical background gives me a unique lens — I understand both the science and the human story behind every prescription.

Applying to Apple Developer Academy to turn that seed into something real. Not just an app — a system of care.

Thesis Research

Antibacterial Gel from
Kawista Fruit Peel

Formulating a natural antibacterial gel from Limonia acidissima L. extract using Ultrasonic-Assisted Extraction — tested against Staphylococcus epidermidis.

01
Phase One
Extraction
UAE Method
  • Ultrasonic-Assisted Extraction (UAE)
  • Frequency: 40 kHz, Temp: 40°C
  • Kawista fruit peel extract
  • Phytochemical screening
02
Phase Two
Antibacterial
Testing
  • Disc diffusion method
  • Target: S. epidermidis
  • Zone of inhibition measurement
  • Minimum inhibitory analysis
03
Phase Three
Pectin
Characterization
  • Pomelo peel pectin extraction
  • TLC characterization
  • Melting point analysis
  • Synthetic vs natural pectin comparison
04
Phase Four
Gel
Formulation
  • 4 gel formula iterations
  • 6 physical parameters evaluated
  • pH, viscosity, spreadability
  • Stability & homogeneity testing
💡
Documented Learning: The natural pectin formula failed to meet all stability parameters. Rather than a setback, this became a rigorous documentation of failure modes, leading to formulation insights that inform future iterations — a methodology I bring into digital product development too.
What I Want to Build

Health App Concept

A family-centered medication companion for elderly Indonesians — designed around the real barriers I witnessed daily as a pharmacy assistant.

👨‍👩‍👧‍👦
Family Loop Notifications
Real-time alerts to family members when doses are missed or drug interactions are detected — keeping everyone in the loop without being intrusive.
🎙️
Voice-First UI for Elderly
Designed for low digital literacy. Voice commands in Bahasa Indonesia for medication logging, reminders, and questions — accessibility by default.
📷
Photo-Scan Medication Input
Point camera at medication packaging to auto-identify drug name, dosage, and instructions — eliminating manual entry errors.
⚠️
Drug Interaction Alerts
Pharmaceutical-grade interaction checking powered by my clinical knowledge — flags dangerous combinations before they become emergencies.
📍
Refill Reminder + Apotek Locator
Predicts refill date based on dose schedule and shows nearest pharmacies (apotek) with opening hours and stock status.

Why This Matters

Indonesia has 29M+ elderly people. Polypharmacy, low health literacy, and fragmented family structures make medication management a silent crisis. This app is designed for the patients I've seen struggle — and the families who want to help but don't know how.
